Shelly’s Summer Camp

A fun, safe summer adventure for kids in Vancouver!

Welcome to Shelly’s Summer Camp!

At Shelly’s Summer Camp, we believe summer should be filled with adventure, creativity, and new friendships. Located in the heart of Vancouver, our camp is designed for kids aged 6–12 who are ready for a summer they’ll never forget!

Our Activities

  • Outdoor Adventures: Nature hikes, scavenger hunts, and outdoor games.
  • Arts & Crafts: Painting, drawing, and creative projects.
  • Sports & Games: Soccer, basketball, relay races, and team-building activities.
  • Special Events: Theme days, talent shows, and campfire storytelling.
  • Educational Workshops: Science experiments, cooking classes, and environmental awareness sessions.

Safety First

  • Trained Staff: All counselors are certified in first aid and child safety.
  • Low Camper-to-Counselor Ratio: Personalized attention for every child.
  • Secure Facilities: Fenced, controlled access for peace of mind.
  • Emergency Protocols: Regular safety drills and clear procedures.
  • Healthy Practices: Nutritious snacks, hydration breaks, and sun safety.

Meet Shelly

Michelle “Shelly” Fitzpatrick is a passionate educator and camp leader with years of experience working with children. Shelly’s warmth, creativity, and dedication make Shelly’s Summer Camp a truly special place for kids to grow, learn, and have fun.
